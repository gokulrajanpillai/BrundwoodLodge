The Lodge is just a short 5 minute drive from the nearby airport.

Come and stay with us in our peaceful B n B accommodation at Stewart Island Lodge. Private and secluded, Stewart Island Lodge is a wonderful place to relax in great comfort while providing a base for your island exploration. The lodge has sweeping views of Halfmoon Bay and the ocean beyond. Its grounds are home to many native birds including kaka (native parrots).
  • Accommodation in King/twin rooms, each with ensuite and bath
  • Extensive gardens
  • Bird watching
  • Continental breakfast included
  • Communal guest lounge with great views over the bay
  • Five minutes walk to village and restaurants
